About Me

I'm Ruth Awino a Software developer

The significance of personal data protection became acutely apparent to me when I found myself as a target of online stalking.
This unsettling encounter prompted me, as a software developer, to immerse myself in the world of technology with a strong focus on privacy.
My journey has been one of continuous learning and gaining experience in privacy-enhancing technologies.
I am driven by the vision of empowering individuals to regain control over their personal information in an increasingly digital world.
By contributing my skills and knowledge, I aspire to create a future where privacy and trust are at the forefront of every digital interaction.
